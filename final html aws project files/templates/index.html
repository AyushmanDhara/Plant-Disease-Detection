PlantGuard aims to revolutionize agricultural disease detection using cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our deep learning model can identify over 38 different plant diseases with high accuracy, helping farmers and gardeners protect their crops and plants effectively.

By providing instant diagnosis and comprehensive treatment recommendations, we empower users to take immediate action and prevent the spread of plant diseases.
